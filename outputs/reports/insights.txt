📊Sales Performance Insights
Technology is the highest revenue-generating category, contributing approximately 4.74M in total sales.
Furniture and Office Supplies follow closely but show lower efficiency in terms of profitability.


💰 Profitability Insights
Office Supplies has the highest average profit margin among all categories.
Furniture shows very low profit margins despite strong sales, indicating cost or discount inefficiencies.


📉 Discount Impact Analysis
Higher discount values are strongly associated with negative profit.
Excessive discounting (above ~0.3) leads to significant losses, highlighting inefficient pricing strategies.


📦 Product Performance
High-end electronic products (Smartphones, Copiers) dominate total sales.
A small group of top products contributes a major share of revenue (Pareto effect).


📈 Sales Trend Analysis
Sales show consistent growth from 2011 to 2014.
Overall business performance is improving year-over-year.


🌍 Regional Performance
Central region leads in total sales.
Africa and West regions also show strong contributions.
Canada has the lowest sales contribution among regions.



🧠 Key Business Conclusion
Revenue growth is strong, but profitability varies significantly across categories.
Discount strategy needs optimization to improve overall profit efficiency.
